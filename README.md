"# WeatherApp" 

A simplistic full screen weather app which obtains current weather information and a 7 day forecast from the National Weather Service and displays it. 

***Can only be used in the United States***

To display weather for your area, change latitude and longitude in the WeatherApp.INI file depicted below to your location & Save. 

        [Location]
        Latitude=45.688867
        Longitude=-122.651783

![Preview](WeatherApp.png)

ALT+F4 exits
